About Us

Our community of students, faculties, and alumni is dedicated to innovation, entrepreneurship, and engineering for social impact.

Our Goals

Welcome to our project finder web page! Our aim is to provide a centralized platform for students to discover and apply for research opportunities in their areas of interest. We understand that finding research projects can be a time-consuming and challenging process, especially for college students who are new to the research world or have limited connections in their field of study. Our project finder web page is designed to streamline this process and help students find research opportunities that align with their academic and career goals. Users can also create their own projects for other people to participate.

Our Expectations

We believe that our project finder web page will enable students to explore their interests, gain valuable research experience, and develop meaningful connections with faculty members and other students in their research area. We are excited to offer this resource and support the research aspirations of college students.
